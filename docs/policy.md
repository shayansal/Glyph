# Policy

Policy is mandatory. It ensures AI cannot create authority, required warnings remain visible and accessible, high-risk actions remain auditable and confirmed, and capability permissions are enforced.

`PolicyStudio` is the developer-facing explanation layer. It evaluates a patch against the current world and last safe world, separates allowed operations from denied operations, carries audit events, and produces human-readable explanations such as: personalization may move a confirmation surface, but cannot hide it or bypass authority.
